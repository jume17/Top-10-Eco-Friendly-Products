Bamboo Water Bottle

-Crafted from natural bamboo and high-quality stainless steel, this eco-conscious water bottle is designed to accompany you on your journey to hydration, ensuring you stay refreshed and make a positive impact on the planet. It is a fast growing grass that requires minimal resources to thrive. The bottles usually consist of a bamboo outer shell and a removable inner glass or stainless-steel lining. This design ensures your beverages come in contact with natural materials rather than plastic, making them an environmentally friendly choice.
