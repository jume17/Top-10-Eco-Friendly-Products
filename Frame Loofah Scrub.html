Loofah Scrub

-Loofah scrubs from the luffa plant have historically been used as scrubbers. Benefits and uses of a loofah include: Some people also swear by loofahs as household cleaning products. They can be used to scrub tiles, showers, sinks, and other hard-to-clean surfaces.The rough, woven texture of loofah scrubs makes them an ideal exfoliating tool. It can help remove dead skin cells and improve circulation. Loofahs also create a rich, bubbly lather when used with soap or body wash. Loofah scrubs have many benefits for both your skin and the environment.
